About us

Noor braiding (NB) salon startd in September 2020. Founded by Fatima Ado Muhammad who specialises in all kinds of mordern and local braiding and has over 5 years experience in the hair care for women. We offer all types of braiding, from , box braids, knotless braids, cornrows. faux locs, passion twists goddess locs, lemonade braids and more. Our team of braiders excel at providing the best experience for our clients in a luxury and relaxing environment.

The braiding salon business is located in the city of Kano, Nigeria. The business was established with the aim of servicing the local clients but now have expanded to cater for distant clients as well. Clients can now book for our services online from the comfort of their homes. They can also follow us on our social media accounts to see more of our services.
